Choose Your Itinerary (Bali)

Day 01: Arrival Bali & Leisure Day in Kuta +
Arrive at the **Ngurah Rai International Airport** in Bali, where you will be met by a representative. You will be transferred to your hotel in **Kuta**, where you check in and can relax for a while. You have the rest of the day at leisure to explore the area, spend time at the beach, or look at the local markets. You can enjoy one of the most beautiful sunsets of your lives. Return to the hotel for a relaxing night.
Day 02: South Bali Tour with Water Sports +
After breakfast, proceed for a **South Bali tour** including **Water Sports activities at Bintang Beach Club**. Activities include **Jet Ski**, **Banana Boat**, and **Fly Fish**. Afterwards, visit **Uluwatu Temple** and **Dreamland Beach**. Return back to the hotel for an overnight stay in Kuta. (Note: Please carry an extra set of clothes.)
Day 03: North Bali Tour +
After breakfast, you will leave for a **North Bali Tour**. Visit **Twin Lake View**, **Ulun Danu Beratan Temple**, **Wanagiri hidden hills**, and **Handara Gate**. Return back to the hotel for an overnight stay in Kuta. (Special Instruction: Start your tour as early as possible, as the Bedugul area often gets cloudy in the afternoon, which might affect your pictures.)
Day 04: O Club by Oculus Kintamani - Mt Batur View Point +
After a delicious breakfast, visit **Tegallalang Rice Terrace**. Then, visit **O Club By Oculus @Kintamani Range** to enjoy the **Warm water swimming pool with Mt Batur View**. (Note: Warm water swimming will start at 3:00 pm. Compulsory Towel and Locker charges of 50,000 IDR per person need to be paid directly to the club. You have a maximum of 2 hours and 30 minutes to spend at O Club.)
Day 05: Kuta to Ubud Transfer & Sightseeing +
After a delicious breakfast, check out from the hotel in Kuta. Today you will be transferred to **Ubud**. Check in to your new hotel. Afterwards, visit **Tanah Lot Temple** and **PicHeaven Bali Swing**. (Please note: The Bali Swing requires an extra dress and photo-shoot payment directly at the site). Evening back to the hotel for an overnight stay in Ubud. (Accommodation: Dedary Resort Ubud / One Bedroom Villa with Private Pool and Bathtub).
Day 06: Leisure Day +
Enjoy your delicious breakfast at the hotel. Spend the day at leisure. You can visit places nearby the hotel or simply relax and enjoy your free time. Overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 07: Departure +
After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Proceed to the airport as per your flight schedule, taking back sweet memories of Bali Island.
